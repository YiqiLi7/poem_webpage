I am for the bright future.


My parents told me that I am light; when I appear in this world, it lights them up; my good friends said to me that I am an eternal light bulb when she connects my wires; my master told me that I was the lamp on the head of a strange fish in the deep sea, which let him see hope in the deepest trench. Oh, they all said that I was the light, the substitute for the sun.
I light them; I warm them. I am the morning sun, the shining star, the moon, the fire, the light from pupils.
